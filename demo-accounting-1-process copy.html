Why do Organisations need a Compliance Management Tool?

Navigating compliance is challenging, but CompSeqr 360 simplifies it for you by:

  • Keeping you updated on changing regulations in real time.

  • Automating tasks like reporting and audits, saving time and effort.

  • Spotting and fixing risks before they become costly.

  • Offering a centralized platform to manage all compliance needs.

Why does Compliance Matter?

Staying compliant isn’t just about avoiding penalties—it’s about building a stronger, more trusted organization. Compliance ensures::

  • Legal Safety: Protects against fines and lawsuits.

  • Brand Trust: Shows customers and investors you follow ethical practices.

  • Operational Efficiency: Reduces errors and boosts productivity.

  • Sustainable Growth: Mitigates risks and supports long-term success.

Types of Compliance Simplify

  • Regulatory Compliance: Adhering to legal standards.

  • Financial Compliance: Transparent and accurate financial processes.

  • Data Privacy: Protecting customer and business data.

  • Environmental Compliance: Meeting eco-friendly obligations.

  • Internal Policies: Streamlining internal processes and ethics.

How will CompSeqr 360 Work for You?

With CompSeqr 360, managing these aspects becomes seamless and efficient, ensuring your business is always prepared for tomorrow.

Our Products and Services

1

Step 1

ProEDox – Document Management System (DMS)

ProEDox is a traditional way to manage compliance documents. Though it’s a manual process to manage compliance documents, it provides framework to manage access control regulation wise, geography wise, entity wise etc.

Benefits:
  • Cost-Effective: Affordable compared to advanced solutions.
  • Simplicity: Easy to set up and manage.
What We Offer:

ProEDox leverages basic DMS functionality by addressing user access management. Access control can be entity-specific or regulation-specific. While primarily for compliance, it can also manage organisation’s other documents also efficiently.

2

Step 2

⁠CaaN – Compliance as an Application

With CompSeqr360, compliance management becomes streamlined and automated, addressing the challenges of traditional manual tracking.

Key Features
  • Real-time compliance tracking.
  • Automated alerts for deadlines and potential gaps.
  • Insights and dashboards for leadership visibility.

3

Step 3

CaaP – Compliance as a Partner

We go beyond software with a comprehensive partnership approach:

1. Full access to CompSeqr360 for compliance tracking.
2. Complete implementation of the system to fit your needs.
3. Ongoing retainership for compliance management.
4. Custom automation of solutions tailored to your unique compliance challenges.

4

Step 4

CaaS – Compliance as a Service

Our professional services team supports your compliance, legal, and risk functions with expert assistance and a network of SME partners.

Services Include:
  • Full access to CompSeqr360.
  • Implementation of compliance solutions.
  • Retainership for ongoing compliance management.
  • Adhoc services like registrations, filing, litigation representation, and audits.
