Become a Subject Matter Expert (SME) Partner with CompSeqr360 and help businesses achieve compliance excellence. Our SME partnership program is designed for experienced professionals who are well-versed in regulations and compliances in their respective countries.

Why Partner with CompSeqr360?

As an SME Partner, you’ll have the opportunity to leverage your expertise while collaborating with a cutting-edge compliance management platform. This partnership allows you to expand your service offerings, provide value-added solutions to clients, and grow your business alongside a trusted global brand.

Eligibility Criteria:

To qualify as an SME Partner, you should meet the following requirements:

  1. Professional Credentials: You must be a qualified CA, CPA, ACCA, CMA, CS, Lawyer, or equivalent professional in your country.
  2. Experience: A minimum of 5-7 years of practice in your domain, with in-depth knowledge of local regulations and compliances.
  3. Team Capacity: Your practice should have a team size of at least five, with the ability to allocate or hire 1-2 full-time resources for CompSeqr360.
  4. Work Environment: A functional office space where your team operates (not remote or WFH setups).
  5. Tech Enthusiasm: A keen interest in leveraging technology to enhance compliance solutions for your clients.

What We Offer:

  • Access to CompSeqr360’s powerful platform to streamline and manage compliance for clients.
  • Training and onboarding for your team to seamlessly integrate CompSeqr360 into your operations.
  • Income Tax: Maintain essential documentation, including Income Tax Returns, Section 12A certificates, and TDS records, for accurate yearly compliance.
  • Collaboration opportunities with CompSeqr’s global network of experts.
  • Revenue-sharing opportunities for services provided using the platform.

Interested? Submit your details in the form below or email us at info@arsi-consulting.com with the subject line “Interested – SME Partner.” Let’s work together to deliver unmatched compliance solutions to businesses across the globe.

Join us as a Sales Partner and become a part of CompSeqr360’s global growth journey! This partnership is an opportunity to offer innovative compliance solutions to businesses in your country while expanding your portfolio and increasing your revenue potential.

Why Partner with CompSeqr360?

As a Sales Partner, you will gain exclusive rights to market, promote, and sell CompSeqr360 in your region. With our advanced SaaS-based compliance management platform and your expertise in software solutions, together we can help businesses streamline their compliance processes, reduce risks, and achieve operational efficiency.

Eligibility Criteria:

To qualify as an SME Partner, you should meet the following requirements:

  1. Software Expertise: Experience in developing, maintaining, or reselling software for at least 3-5 years.
  2. Technological Knowledge: Familiarity with the latest technological advancements in compliance and business management.
  3. ERP Familiarity: Understanding of Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems and other applications businesses use to manage data or content.
  4. Market Reach: Established network and market presence to reach potential clients across industries.

What We Offer:

  • Comprehensive training on CompSeqr360’s features and benefits.
  • Marketing and promotional support to help you succeed.
  • Ongoing technical and operational assistance for smooth implementation.
  • Collaboration opportunities with CompSeqr’s global network of experts.
  • Attractive incentives and revenue-sharing opportunities.

Interested? Submit your details in the form below or send an email to info@arsi-consulting.com with the subject line “Interested – Sales Partner.” Together, let’s redefine compliance management for businesses worldwide.
